Event Postponed

Dear Runners,

We hope this message finds you well. We are writing with a heavy heart to inform you that the Halwa City Marathon 2025, originally scheduled for June 15, 2025, has been postponed due to the tragic Air India flight crash.

As a community, we stand in solidarity with those affected by this heartbreaking incident. Out of respect and due to logistical concerns, we have decided to reschedule the marathon to a later date.

We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and appreciate your understanding during this time. All registrations will remain valid for the rescheduled event, and your chest number and details will remain unchanged.

We will notify you of the new event date soon via email and social media.

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to us at halwacitymarathon@gmail.com or call +91 9500415515.
